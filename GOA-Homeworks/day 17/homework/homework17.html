Lamborghini Urus

Overview

The Lamborghini Urus is the world’s first Super Sport Utility Vehicle, merging the soul of a super sports car with the functionality of an SUV. Born from a fusion of Lamborghini’s peerless engineering prowess and luxury heritage, the Urus is a testament to uncompromising design and performance.

Performance

Under the hood of the Urus lies a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, delivering 650 horsepower and 850 Nm of torque. This allows the Urus to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.6 seconds, with a top speed of 305 km/h. The Urus also boasts advanced driving dynamics, adaptive air suspension, and active torque vectoring to ensure an unparalleled driving experience.
